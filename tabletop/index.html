Tabletop game is a general term used to refer to board games, card games, dice games, miniatures wargames, tile-based games and other games that are normally played on a table or other flat surface.

The term is used to distinguish these types of games from sports and video games. The term is also used to distinguish role-playing games from role-playing video games and LARPs, although role-playing games may not necessarily require a wide playing surface.

Here are a few examples of tabletop games I've designed:

Time Chess
A forth-dimensional spin on the classic game of chess. The basic premise of the game is: how would chess be different if your pieces were allowed to travel through time?

By The Seat of Your Pants
