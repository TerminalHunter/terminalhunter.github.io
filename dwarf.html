Hunter Dewing, "TerminalHunter", programmer  

Hunter Dewing has been quite content lately. He admired a fine Statue lately. He has been exhausted lately. He had a pretty decent drink lately. He made a friend recently. He was irritated by the sun lately.
He is a casual worshipper of the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
He is a citizen of The United States. He is a former member of The University of Virginia. He is a former member of The Student Game Developers at The University of Virginia. He is the former president of The Brony Club at the University of Virginia. He arrived at Tidewater on the 20th of Felsite in the year 2014.
He is years old, born on the 21st of Malachite in the year 1992.
He is tall. His eyes are green. His hair is straight. His long hair is neatly combed. His hair is dirty blonde.
He is agile and virtually never sick, but he is quick to tire and flimsy.
Hunter Dewing likes rose quartz, two-handed swords, the color purple, small horses for their friendship, and cats for their aloofness. When possible, he prefers to consume pig, honeyed pretzels, and hard cider. He absolutely detests silverfish.
He has awesome intellectual powers, an iron will, good creativity, a deep well of patience, and an amazing memory, but he has a little difficulty with words and next to no natural musical ability.
Like others in his culture, he deeply respects skill at arms, thinks it is of the utmost importance to present a bold face and never grouse, complain, or even show emotion, sees friendship as one of the finer things in life, values family greatly, values self control, values loyalty, respects fair-dealing and fair-play, finds merrymaking and partying worthwhile activities, respects the development of skill, values good craftsmanship, respects the law, values leisure time, values independence, values hard work, and respects perseverance. He personally holds well-laid plans and shrewd deceptions in the highest regard, feels that introspection and all forms of self-examination are the keys to a good life and worthy of respect, sees cooperation as very important in life, finds the following of tradition foolish and limiting, doesn't particularly respect commerce, and values peace over war. He dreams of crafting a masterwork video game someday.
He does not generally respond to emotional appeals. He doesn't mind a little tumult and discord in day-to-day living. He has a calm demeanor. He tries to keep his things orderly. He plays with his facial hair when he's thinking.

A medium-sized creature prone to great ambition.
